Notes:

The Master Mobile UX videos were very interesting and informative. I was interested in how many people still don't recognize the "hamburger menu" as a clickable item. I always thought this was intuitive and made the page very clean; however, I ame reconsidering my opinion on it. If I design a page where only a limited number of people understand how to move around in it, people will not utilize the page I made.

It was also interesting to listen to how mobile devices have changed over the years and the design process in creating for mobile devices. I for one was very frustrated in apples latest ios release for the iPhone. I do not like how it defaults to the search bar at the bottom of the page in Safari. I can see why they did this so more users can reach it easier. This is especially good for iPad or iPhone plus users who have a large screen. I personally am not a fan of it and moved it back to the top.
